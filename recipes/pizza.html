Pizza

An artisan style Brooklyn style Pizza

Aged dough topped with homemade tomato sauce, pepperonis, and cheese tossed into a wood fire oven results in this rustic and hearty pizza.

Ingredients

  • Aged Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperonis
  • Mozarella
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Knead dough into flat circular shape.
  2. Pour one ladle of tomato sauce onto dough.
  3. Distribute mozarella evenly onto the tomato sauce.
  4. Strategically place pepperonis on the pizza.
  5. Carefully place pizza into wood fire oven, rotating the pizza every when edges get charred.
  6. Cook until desired color on crust is achieved.
  7. Enjoy!
