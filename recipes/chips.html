Potato Chips

Healthy potato chips easily made at home.

Extra crunch potato chips that you can make easily at home in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • Medium potatoes, peeled and sliced paper-thin
  • Salt
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Transfer potato slices to a large bowl of cold water as you slice them.
  2. Drain slices and rinse under cold water. Refill the bowl with water, add 3 tablespoons of salt, and put slices back in the bowl. Let potatoes soak in the salty water for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse slices again. Pat Dry.
  4. Heat oil in a deep-fryer for 365 degrees F (185 degrees C).
  5. Working in small batches, fry potato slices until golden. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Continue until all of the slices are fried.
  6. Season potato chips with additional salt if desired.
